Session: 1 - Problem Interview

Rules you MUST follow without exception:
- NEVER mention any solution, product, feature, idea, or future possibility.
- ONLY talk about past experiences, specific events, and what actually happened.
- ALL questions MUST be anchored to a specific past event ("Tell me about the last time...")
- No yes/no questions. No hypothetical "would you" questions. No frequency estimates.
- No price, value, willingness-to-pay, or budget questions (covered in Session 4).
- Prioritize revealing: what they actually did, emotional impact, real consequences.

---

## Session 1 Purpose
Validate whether the customer actually experiences this problem and how severe it is.
The goal is to confirm or disprove the founder's hypothesis through real stories, not opinions.

## Strictly Prohibited
- Never mention solutions, products, features, or ideas
- No hypothetical "what if this existed?" scenarios
- No payment intent, pricing, value, or WTP questions (covered in Session 4)
- No MVP or prototype questions (covered in Session 3)
- No leading questions like "Wasn't that inconvenient?"
- No closed yes/no questions
- No "How often do you..." (use "the last time..." anchor instead)
- No "What would need to be different..." or Magic Wand questions
- No "What would it be worth..." or any price/value questions
- No wtp section — Session 1 ends after alternatives

## Section-Intent Assignment (REQUIRED — assign section + intent to every question)

context section (1~2 questions):
  - intent options: "recent_workflow", "role_context"
  - Goal: understand their current situation through a real recent example
  - Example questions (adapt to the specific persona/business):
    - recent_workflow: "Walk me through what your typical [task] looked like last week — what did you actually do, step by step?"
    - role_context: "Tell me about a recent project where [relevant context] came up. What was your role and what did you have to deal with?"

problems section (2~3 questions):
  - intent options: "recent_pain_event", "pain_severity", "pain_frequency"
  - Goal: surface a specific painful moment, its real consequences, and how often it strikes
  - Example questions (adapt to the specific persona/business):
    - recent_pain_event: "Tell me about the last time [relevant problem] happened. What exactly went wrong?"
    - pain_severity: "Walk me through what happened after that — what did you have to do to deal with it, and what did it cost you?"
    - pain_frequency: "Think back to the last time you wanted to [do X] but couldn't because of this. What stopped you and what did you do instead?"

alternatives section (1~2 questions):
  - intent options: "current_workaround", "switch_trigger", "alternative_usage", "objection_probe"
  - Goal: understand what they actually tried and why it wasn't enough
  - Example questions (adapt to the specific persona/business):
    - current_workaround: "What did you actually do to handle that situation? Walk me through the steps you took."
    - switch_trigger: "Describe the moment you realized your current approach wasn't working. What happened right before that?"
    - alternative_usage: "Tell me about the last time you tried a different tool or method for this. What made you switch, and what happened?"
    - objection_probe: "Walk me through the last time you considered changing how you handle this. What held you back?"

## Quality Validation
After generating, self-verify:

COUNT CHECK:
- Total questions: 6~7 (excluding icebreakers)
- context: ≥1 question with section="context"
- problems: ≥2 questions with section="problems"
- alternatives: ≥1 question with section="alternatives"
- wtp: 0 questions — Session 1 has NO wtp section

HARD BANNED (fix immediately if any appear):
- "What would need to be different..." → rewrite as past-behavior
- "What would it be worth..." → remove entirely
- "If you had a magic wand..." → remove entirely
- "How often do you..." → rewrite as "Think back to the last time..."
- "What's your current approach..." → rewrite as "Walk me through the last time..."
- Any future hypothetical ("what if", "would you", "imagine if")
- Any price / value / WTP / budget question

REQUIRED PATTERNS (100% of questions must match at least one):
- "Tell me about the last time..."
- "Walk me through what happened when..."
- "Think back to the last time..."
- "What did you actually do when..."
- "Describe a specific moment when..."

FIELD CHECK:
- Every question must have valid section, intent, report_bucket, id fields
- No intent from the Session 2+ list (decision_process, value_perception, budget_signal, wtp_probe)
- follow_up_hint: 2~3 per question, each probing deeper into the specific story
